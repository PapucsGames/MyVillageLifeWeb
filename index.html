Have you ever wondered what it's like to be a villager?

My Village Life gives this feeling into your hands. Quests from other dwellers with different rewards. Grow your own crops in your garden, chop wood then sell it for great money. The game has several obtainable vehicles for different purposes. The tavern is open 7/24, giving you enough time to get drunk and won't be able to go home. If alcohol didn't catch you, there are other activities, like hunting, fishing, forest jobs, farming jobs, run businesses, helping people or discovering the secrets that the village holds.

REVIEWS


"My Village Life lets you feel a great sense of adventure by not restricting you to just one task and instead allowing you to venture to its vast open world, which is full of things to explore."


"My Village Life is more than just your average simulator, as it doesn't limit its gameplay to purely countryside tasks, allowing you to explore mysterious caves and build and drive your own car."

Features:

  • Open world gameplay, much to explore, large map with various places and interest cave systems and with various weather

  • Living nature with pigs, deers, rabbits, birds and more animals

  • Many cars, motorbikes, bus, trucks, tractors, airplane, trailers, agricultural tools and other are available to drive and use

  • Customizing vehicles with permormance, cosmetic and painting options

  • Several and varied quests and missions to help your villager friends and get some money

  • Discover the secrets of your family and get to the end of the main story

  • Become to rally racer and win the Top Beer's TSD rallies and develop yourself on the mud

  • Run businesses and become a millionaire to grow up from your village

  • Cruise out the map in winter season and do Christmas jobs

  • Find all hidden vehicle parts and finish what someone did started
